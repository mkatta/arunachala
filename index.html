About Us

Rooted in Dharma,
Serving with Devotion

Arunachala Foundation is a charitable trust committed to the revitalisation of Hindu Dharma. Named after the sacred Arunachala Hill — revered as the embodiment of Lord Shiva — our foundation draws inspiration from the timeless wisdom of the Vedas, Upanishads, and the teachings of great sages.

We believe that Dharma is not merely a set of rituals but a living force that sustains harmony in the individual, the family, and the cosmos. Through charitable service, education, and temple support, we strive to make this eternal knowledge accessible to all.

What We Do

Our Pillars of Service

Every action rooted in Dharma creates ripples of positive change across generations.

Temple Restoration

Renovating and maintaining ancient temples, preserving sacred architecture and worship traditions for future generations.

Vedic Education

Supporting Veda Pāṭhaśālās, Sanskrit learning centres, and initiatives that keep the sacred knowledge alive.

Community Seva

Organising annadānam (food distribution), medical camps, and welfare programmes for devotees and underprivileged families.

Festival Support

Facilitating grand celebrations of festivals like Maha Shivaratri, Karthigai Deepam, and other sacred observances.

Goshala & Environment

Maintaining goshalas (cow shelters) and promoting environmental stewardship as taught in our scriptures.

Youth Awareness

Engaging young Hindus through workshops, discussions, and digital content to connect with their roots.

Scripture Publishing

Publishing and freely distributing sacred texts, translations, and commentaries in regional languages.
